Our focus

Three guiding goals

Identify

Surface the real barriers

Diagnose inequality, diversity and inclusion issues specific to CABD to ensure responses address lived realities.

Communicate

Create safe channels

Maintain transparent pathways to report inequity and share our activities with the scientific community.

Act

Enable equal opportunities

Promote actions that secure fair access, inclusive culture and zero tolerance to harassment.

How we work

From policy to practice

Support & Reporting
  • Confidential guidance for anyone facing discrimination or harassment.
  • Connect cases to the updated CSIC harassment protocol and campus resources.
  • Psychological support and follow-up through partner services.
Training & Awareness
  • Workshops on bias, inclusive leadership and bystander action.
  • Sessions on integrating sex/gender analysis into research design.
  • Annual events around 11F, 8M, Pride Month and Cultural Diversity Day.
Policy & Data
  • Monitor representation across career stages and publish dashboards.
  • Coordinate with UPO Equality Office, CSIC Women & Science Commission and SomMa EDI WG.
  • Align centre procedures with CSIC and national equality plans.
